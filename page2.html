How to get there

There are two train stations.
1) From hillview mrt station, exit via A and take a 5 minutes walk to Dairy Farm Road where you can begin your journey to the Dairy Farm Nature Park.
2) From beauty world mart station, exit via B and take a 8 minutes walk to Hindhede Road to start your journey to the Bukit Timah Nature Reserve.
